## About

**It's Already When.** is a weekly cybersecurity intelligence digest written for people who don't need the hype explained — just the signal.

Every week: the breaches that mattered, the vulnerabilities worth patching, the threat actors worth watching, and the trends worth understanding. No vendor fluff. No fear-mongering. Just what happened and why it matters.

*Published weekly. Written by someone who's been watching too long.*

---

### Sources

This digest draws from publicly available threat intelligence, security advisories, vendor disclosures, and industry reporting. Sources include but are not limited to CISA advisories, NVD/CVE feeds, and major security publications.
